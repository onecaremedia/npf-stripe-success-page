How billing works

  • 1 We securely save your card. You're not charged today.
  • 2 After each service, we charge your estimated cost to your card on file.
  • 3 Your insurer reviews the claim and may adjust the final cost (for example, based on your deductible).
  • 4 If you were overcharged, we refund the difference. If undercharged, we email you, then charge the balance.

How we estimate your cost

Your estimate is our best projection of what you'll pay, based on what we know today about your insurance plan's benefits and the services you're receiving. The final cost usually matches or comes close.

Why your cost may change

Occasionally the final cost differs from the estimate, because some information can't be confirmed until your insurer processes the claim — for example, changes to your deductible, plan resets, or unexpected plan details.

Auto corrections

You're automatically refunded if you were overcharged, or charged the balance if you were undercharged — no need to remember a separate payment. We'll always email you at least 3 days before any correction charge, including the date, amount, and the card on file.
